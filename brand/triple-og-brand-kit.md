## 3. Color Palette

### Primary Colors (from actual packaging)
| Name | Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Matte Black | Primary background | `#0A0A0A` | Box packaging, IG backgrounds, dark UI |
| Neon Green | Logo accent, highlights | `#4AE54A` | OG logo outline, CTAs, accent text |
| Purple / Violet | Brand accent | `#8B2FC9` | "TRIPLE" text, secondary highlights |
| White | Info text, contrast | `#FFFFFF` | Product specs, body text on dark |

### Secondary Colors
| Name | Role | Hex |
|------|------|-----|
| Charcoal | Dark UI backgrounds | `#1C1C1C` |
| Smoke Gray | Borders, muted text | `#666666` |
| Dark Green | Hover/pressed state | `#2BA32B` |
| Dark Purple | Hover/pressed state | `#6B1FA9` |

### Color Rules
- **Default background is BLACK.** This brand lives on dark backgrounds.
- Neon green is for **emphasis only** -- never for large text blocks
- Purple is the secondary accent -- use for variety, not dominance
- White text on black for readability
- Never use neon green on white backgrounds (low contrast)

## 5. Brand Voice

### Personality
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| OG | Street credibility, been in the game, knows the work |
| Direct | No fluff. Trimmers don't have time for marketing speak. |
| Value-forward | Price and quantity are features, not afterthoughts |
| Industry insider | Speaks the language -- trim, harvest, trichomes, terps |
| Not corporate | Never sounds like a press release. Sounds like a trim lead. |

### Voice Examples

**DO:**
- "trim never sleeps. your gloves better keep up."
- "cheap gloves rip. period."
- "5mil nitrile. built for the marathon, not the sprint."
- "1 case = 10 boxes = 1,000 gloves. that's a LOT of trim sessions."

**DON'T:**
- "Our premium nitrile examination gloves are engineered for..." (too corporate)
- "Yo check out these fire gloves bruh" (too try-hard)
- "Save money with our affordable solution!" (generic, not industry)

### Tone by Context
| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Instagram captions | Lowercase, punchy, culture-insider. Short lines. |
| Product pages | Spec-forward, factual, concise |
| Email outreach | Professional but casual. B2B but human. |
| Reel end cards | Bold, minimal. Brand name + URL only. |

## 7. Imagery & Visual Direction

### Product Photography
- Triple OG box on matte black surface
- Dramatic side lighting (green/purple gel optional)
- Gloved hand pulling from box
- Single glove stretched/snapped
- Resin-covered gloves after trim session (authenticity)

### Lifestyle / Scene Shots
- Trim rooms: long tables, work lights, crew at stations
- Grow rooms: LED lights, canopy shots, plant inspection
- Warehouse: cases stacked, supply shelves, delivery
- Harvest: cutting colas, drying lines, trim table setup

### What to Avoid
- Faces (legal/privacy in cannabis industry)
- Recreational cannabis use imagery
- Stock photo aesthetic
- Bright/cheerful color palettes
- Corporate product photography on white seamless
